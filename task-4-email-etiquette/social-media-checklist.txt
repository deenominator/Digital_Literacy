Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)
Do’s
* Think before you post anything online
* Maintain a respectful and positive tone
* Protect your personal information (privacy settings)
* Share useful and meaningful content
* Build a professional online presence
________________


 Don’ts
* Don’t share sensitive personal details publicly
* Don’t engage in online arguments or hate speech
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
* Don’t believe or spread fake news without verifying
* Don’t overshare about your personal life
